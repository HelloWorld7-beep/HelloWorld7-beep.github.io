Synthetic Biology Meets Generative AI: Extending Diffusion Models to DNA Sequences for Programmable CSLA Enzyme Generation


August 2024 - Present

Principle Investigator: Dr. Catalin Voiniciuc
Institution: University of Florida
Department: Horticultural Sciences


Research Focus:

Currently, I am a part of the AI Scholars program at the University of Florida, where I am working under Professor Voiniciuc at the Designer Glycans Lab. My project is "Synthetic Biology Meets Generative AI: Extending Diffusion Models to DNA Sequences for Programmable CSLA Enzyme Generation" . I am researching how to computationally generate CSLA enzymes with programmable activities, using our lab's collected data of the structures and functions of such enzymes.


Responsibilities:

Thus far I have not yet started the project, so I have not done much besides reading articles and planning.
