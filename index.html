Research

Currently, I am a part of the AI Scholars program at the University of Florida, where I am working under Professor Voiniciuc at the Designer Glycans Lab. My project is "Synthetic Biology Meets Generative AI: Extending Diffusion Models to DNA Sequences for Programmable CSLA Enzyme Generation". In other words, I am attempting to computationally generate CSLA enzymes with programmable activities, using our lab's collected data of the structures and functions of such enzymes.
